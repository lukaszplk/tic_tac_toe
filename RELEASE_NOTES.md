## ðŸŽ® How to Play

1. **Player 1 (X)** starts first with red X's
2. **Player 2 (O)** follows with blue O's
3. Click any empty square to place your mark
4. Get three in a row (horizontal, vertical, or diagonal) to win!
5. After game ends, view scores and click to play again
